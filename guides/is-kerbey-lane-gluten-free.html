🥞 Is Kerbey Lane Gluten Free?

Everything you need to know about eating gluten-free at Kerbey Lane Cafe

Home > Guides > Is Kerbey Lane Gluten Free?

📅 Last Updated: March 20, 2026

✅ Yes, Kerbey Lane Has Good GF Options!

Kerbey Lane offers GF pancakes, GF buns for burgers/sandwiches, and many naturally GF items. Plus they're open 24 hours at some locations!

Kerbey Lane Cafe—the Austin diner institution known for quirky decor, generous portions, and 24-hour service—is surprisingly gluten-free friendly. Unlike many diners, Kerbey Lane actively accommodates celiacs with GF pancakes, GF buns, and knowledgeable staff. It's one of Austin's best late-night GF options.

🥞 What's Gluten-Free at Kerbey Lane?

✅ Gluten-Free Menu Items

🥞 Gluten-Free Pancakes (MUST-TRY!)

Kerbey Lane offers gluten-free pancakes that are legitimately good—fluffy, tasty, and satisfying. You can get them as a stack or add them to breakfast combos.

💡 Pro Tip: Order the GF pancakes with fresh berries and whipped cream. They're made fresh to order.

Gluten-Free Bun (For Burgers & Sandwiches):

  • Available for any burger or sandwich ($2-3 upcharge typically)
  • Good quality bun that holds together
  • Staff toast GF buns separately from regular buns

Breakfast (Naturally GF or Modified):

  • Eggs (any style)
  • Bacon, sausage patties
  • Hash browns (verify cooking method—cross-contamination risk)
  • Fresh fruit
  • Omelets (tons of options)
  • Breakfast tacos on corn tortillas (verify availability)

Burgers & Sandwiches (On GF Bun):

  • Any burger with GF bun
  • Grilled chicken sandwich on GF bun
  • Many modifications possible

Salads & Lighter Options:

  • Most salads are naturally GF (skip croutons)
  • Veggie plates
  • Many Tex-Mex items can be made GF (corn tortillas)

❌ Avoid (Contain Gluten)

  • Regular pancakes, waffles, French toast
  • Regular buns (obviously)
  • Breaded chicken tenders
  • Anything described as "crispy" or "fried"

⚠️ Cross-Contamination Considerations

Kerbey Lane is a shared kitchen with gluten everywhere, but they have better protocols than most diners:

  • GF buns are toasted separately
  • GF pancakes are made on a clean section of the griddle (request this explicitly)
  • Staff are trained on celiac accommodations (varies by location/server)
  • Hash browns may be cooked in shared oil—ask about cooking method

💡 How to order safely: "I have celiac disease. I'd like GF pancakes cooked on a clean griddle, please."

🌙 Why Kerbey Lane is Great for GF Diners

✓ 24-Hour Locations: Several Kerbey Lane locations are open 24/7—perfect for late-night cravings or early-morning flights.

✓ Actual GF Options: Unlike Whataburger or P. Terry's, Kerbey Lane invested in GF pancakes and buns. They care about accommodating dietary restrictions.

✓ Breakfast Anytime: Order pancakes and eggs at midnight if you want. It's a diner!

✓ Local Austin Institution: Been around since 1980. Staff are generally experienced with dietary needs.

✓ Affordable: $10-15 per person typically—reasonably priced for the quality and portions.

🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kerbey Lane Cafe gluten free?

Kerbey Lane has good gluten-free options including GF pancakes, GF buns, and many naturally GF breakfast/lunch items. It's not a GF facility (shared kitchen), but they accommodate celiacs better than most diners.

Does Kerbey Lane have gluten-free pancakes?

Yes! Kerbey Lane offers gluten-free pancakes that are actually good. Request they be cooked on a clean section of the griddle if you have celiac.

Does Kerbey Lane have gluten-free buns?

Yes. Kerbey Lane has gluten-free buns available for burgers and sandwiches (small upcharge). They toast them separately from regular buns.

Is Kerbey Lane open 24 hours?

Several locations are 24 hours, making Kerbey Lane one of Austin's best late-night GF options. Verify your specific location's hours—not all are 24/7.

Is Kerbey Lane safe for celiac disease?

Yes, with communication. Inform staff about celiac, request clean griddle for pancakes, verify bun is toasted separately. Kerbey Lane is safer than most diners and accommodates celiacs regularly.

💡 Final Verdict

Kerbey Lane is one of Austin's best GF-friendly diners. The GF pancakes are legit, the buns are available, and the 24-hour locations make it perfect for late-night or early-morning meals. Much better than Whataburger or P. Terry's for GF dining.

Best for: Late-night munchies, breakfast anytime, affordable family meals, when you want diner food that actually accommodates GF needs.
