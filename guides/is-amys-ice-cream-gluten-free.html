🍦 Is Amy's Ice Cream Gluten Free?

Everything you need to know about eating gluten-free at Amy's Ice Cream

Home > Guides > Is Amy's Ice Cream Gluten Free?

📅 Last Updated: March 20, 2026

✅ Yes, Most Amy's Flavors Are GF!

Amy's Ice Cream has many gluten-free flavors made from scratch daily. Avoid cookie/brownie flavors and request clean scoops.

Amy's Ice Cream—the Austin icon known for mixing toppings on a frozen slab, creative flavors, and late-night South Congress vibes—is a frequent question from GF Austinites. The good news: most Amy's flavors are naturally gluten-free. The catch: cross-contamination risk from mix-ins like cookies and brownies.

🍦 What's Gluten-Free at Amy's Ice Cream?

✅ Naturally Gluten-Free Base Flavors

Most of Amy's ice cream flavors are GF because the base is simple: cream, sugar, natural flavorings. Generally safe flavors include:

  • Vanilla, Chocolate, Strawberry — Classic bases, naturally GF
  • Mexican Vanilla — One of Amy's most popular, GF
  • Mint — Naturally GF
  • Fruit sorbets — Dairy-free AND gluten-free
  • Coffee, caramel, nut-based flavors — Typically GF

❌ Avoid These Flavors (Contain Gluten)

  • Any flavor with cookies, brownies, or cake mixed in
  • Cookies & Cream — Contains Oreo pieces
  • Birthday Cake, Cookie Dough — Obvious gluten
  • Any flavor with visible chunks — Ask what they are before ordering

⚠️ Toppings to Avoid

Amy's is famous for mixing in toppings on the frozen slab. Avoid these mix-ins:

  • Oreos, cookies, brownies, cake pieces
  • Waffle cone pieces
  • Graham crackers
  • Anything with "crunch" that might be cookie-based

Safe mix-ins: Fresh fruit, nuts, chocolate chips (verify), caramel, fudge, sprinkles (verify no malt).

⚠️ Cross-Contamination at Amy's

The reality: Amy's scoops ice cream with the same utensils that just scooped cookies & cream. Cookie crumbs are all over the toppings bar. Cross-contamination risk is HIGH unless you take precautions.

How to Order Safely with Celiac:

  1. Ask for a clean scoop: "I have celiac disease. Can you please use a clean scoop from the back?"
  2. Order simple flavors: Mexican Vanilla, Chocolate, Strawberry—nothing with visible chunks
  3. Skip mix-ins entirely: Don't risk cookie/brownie cross-contamination on the frozen slab
  4. Cup only, no cone: Waffle cones contain gluten
  5. Watch the scooping: Make sure they use a truly clean scoop

🍦 Alternative: 100% Gluten-Free Ice Cream

Want zero cross-contamination worry? These Austin ice cream shops are safer for celiacs:

  • Lick Honest Ice Creams — Many GF flavors, very transparent about ingredients
  • Van Leeuwen Ice Cream — Clearly labeled GF flavors
  • Buy pints at grocery stores — Many brands (Ben & Jerry's, Häagen-Dazs) label GF flavors clearly

🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

Is Amy's Ice Cream gluten free?

Many flavors are gluten-free, including Mexican Vanilla, Chocolate, Strawberry, and fruit sorbets. Avoid flavors with cookies, brownies, or cake. Cross-contamination from shared scoops is a concern—request clean scoops.

Is Amy's Mexican Vanilla gluten free?

Yes! Amy's Mexican Vanilla is one of their most popular flavors and is naturally gluten-free.

Does Amy's Ice Cream have gluten-free cones?

No. Amy's waffle cones contain gluten. Order in a cup only.

Is Amy's Ice Cream safe for celiac disease?

With caution, yes. Many flavors are GF by ingredients, but cross-contamination from shared scoops is a risk. Always request a clean scoop from the back if you have celiac.

💡 Final Verdict

✅ Amy's Works for GF Diners (With Precautions)

For celiac disease: ⚠️ Possible with clean scoop and simple flavors. Cross-contamination risk exists.

For mild gluten sensitivity: ✅ Generally fine. Most flavors are GF.

Our take: Amy's is an Austin treasure, and many flavors are naturally GF. Just be smart about ordering—request clean scoops, avoid cookie flavors, and you'll be fine. It's worth visiting for the experience, but if you're highly sensitive, buy pre-packaged GF pints instead.
