üìã Tips for Ordering GF Breakfast

At Restaurants with GF Options (Not Dedicated GF)

  • Communicate clearly: Tell your server "I have celiac disease" or "I need this gluten-free due to an allergy"
  • Ask about shared surfaces: Griddles, toasters, and prep areas may have gluten residue
  • Request fresh oil: For pancakes/waffles, ask if they can use fresh oil or a clean pan
  • Check sauces and dressings: Many contain hidden gluten (soy sauce, Worcestershire, etc.)
  • Verify toppings: Some crumbles, granolas, and toppings contain gluten

Best Naturally GF Breakfast Options

When in doubt, these breakfast items are typically safe:

  • Eggs (scrambled, fried, poached, omelets)
  • Bacon and sausage (verify no flour fillers)
  • Fresh fruit
  • Hash browns/potatoes (ask about shared fryer)
  • Avocado
  • Yogurt with fresh fruit (skip granola unless verified GF)
  • Corn tortillas for breakfast tacos

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I get gluten-free pancakes in Austin?

Bouldin Creek Cafe is the go-to for GF pancakes and waffles. Snooze AM Eatery also offers GF pancakes at multiple locations. For 100% GF options, The Well occasionally has grain-free pancakes on their weekend menu.

Are there gluten-free biscuits in Austin?

Yes! Bird Bird Biscuit is famous for their GF biscuit option. It's prepared in a dedicated area and can be paired with any of their delicious sandwich fillings. It's hands-down the best GF biscuit in Austin.

Where can I find gluten-free donuts in Austin?

Bougie's Donuts & Coffee is 100% gluten-free‚Äîthe safest option. Bom Bakeshop offers dedicated GF mochi donuts. Zucchini Kill Bakery has vegan GF donuts on rotation.

What's the safest breakfast spot for celiac disease?

For zero cross-contamination risk, choose 100% GF restaurants: Bougie's Donuts, Picnik, The Well, or Bom Bakeshop. These establishments don't have any gluten in their kitchens.

Do Austin breakfast spots offer GF toast?

Some do! The Well offers grain-free bread. Flower Child has GF bread for their avocado toast. Many spots can substitute corn tortillas for toast. Always verify the bread is stored and toasted separately.
