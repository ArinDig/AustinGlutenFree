🥞 Is Magnolia Cafe Gluten Free?

Everything you need to know about eating gluten-free at Magnolia Cafe

Home > Guides > Is Magnolia Cafe Gluten Free?

📅 Last Updated: March 20, 2026

⚠️ Some GF Options, But Limited

Magnolia Cafe has some naturally GF items (omelets, salads), but no GF pancakes or buns. Cross-contamination risk in shared diner kitchen.

Magnolia Cafe—the quirky 24-hour Austin diner known for "Sorry, We're Open" sign, massive pancakes, and South Congress location—is an Austin institution. For GF diners: Magnolia has limited options. No GF pancakes or buns, and the shared diner kitchen makes cross-contamination a concern.

🥞 What's Gluten-Free at Magnolia?

  • ✅ Omelets (verify no pancake batter mixed in eggs—some diners do this)
  • ✅ Scrambled eggs, bacon, sausage
  • ✅ Salads (skip croutons)
  • ✅ Some Tex-Mex items on corn tortillas (verify availability)
  • ❌ Pancakes, waffles (no GF versions)
  • ❌ Buns, toast (no GF options)

💡 Better 24-Hour GF Option

🏆 Kerbey Lane Cafe (24hr + GF Friendly!)

Why it's better: Kerbey Lane has GF pancakes and GF buns, plus better celiac protocols. Also 24 hours at some locations.

🙋 FAQ

Does Magnolia Cafe have gluten-free pancakes?

No. Magnolia Cafe does not offer gluten-free pancakes.

Is Magnolia Cafe safe for celiac?

Limited. Some naturally GF items exist (eggs, salads), but no GF buns/pancakes and high cross-contamination risk. Kerbey Lane is a better 24hr GF diner choice.
