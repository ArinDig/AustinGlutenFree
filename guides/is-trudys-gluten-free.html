🍹 Is Trudy's Gluten Free?

Everything you need to know about eating gluten-free at Trudy's Texas Star

Home > Guides > Is Trudy's Gluten Free?

📅 Last Updated: March 20, 2026

✅ Yes, Trudy's Has Good GF Options!

Trudy's offers corn tortillas, many naturally GF Tex-Mex items, and their famous Mexican Martinis are GF!

Trudy's Texas Star—the Austin Tex-Mex staple known for legendary Mexican Martinis, stuffed avocados, and multiple Austin locations—is beloved by locals. For GF diners: Trudy's has solid options with corn tortillas, naturally GF proteins, and accommodating staff. Similar to Chuy's in safety profile.

🌮 What's Gluten-Free at Trudy's?

✅ Safe to Order

🍹 Mexican Martinis (GF!)

Trudy's most famous item—the Mexican Martini—is gluten-free! Tequila, orange liqueur, lime, olive juice. One of Austin's most iconic drinks.

  • ✅ Corn tortillas (for tacos, enchiladas, fajitas)
  • ✅ Fajitas with corn tortillas (chicken, beef, shrimp)
  • ✅ Enchiladas on corn tortillas (verify sauce)
  • ✅ Tacos with grilled proteins on corn tortillas
  • ✅ Stuffed Avocado (verify ingredients—usually GF)
  • ✅ Queso (verify no flour thickener)
  • ✅ Guacamole, salsa
  • ⚠️ Chips (verify dedicated fryer)

⚠️ Cross-Contamination

Trudy's is a shared kitchen—corn and flour tortillas on same griddle, potential for cross-contact. Inform staff about celiac and request clean prep.

🙋 FAQ

Is Trudy's gluten free?

Trudy's has good GF options including corn tortillas, fajitas, enchiladas, and their famous Mexican Martinis. Cross-contamination is possible—communicate with staff.

Are Trudy's Mexican Martinis gluten free?

Yes! Mexican Martinis are GF (tequila-based). One of Austin's most iconic drinks and safe for celiacs.
