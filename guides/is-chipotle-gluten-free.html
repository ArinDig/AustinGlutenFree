🌯 Is Chipotle Gluten Free?

Everything you need to know about eating gluten-free at Chipotle Mexican Grill

Home > Guides > Is Chipotle Gluten Free?

📅 Last Updated: March 20, 2026

✅ YES! Chipotle is Celiac-Friendly

Chipotle is one of the best fast-food chains for gluten-free dining. Almost the entire menu is naturally GF—just skip flour tortillas!

Chipotle Mexican Grill is the gold standard for gluten-free fast food. All proteins, rice, beans, salsas, and toppings are naturally gluten-free—only flour tortillas, crispy taco shells, and one salad dressing contain gluten. With proper ordering (requesting glove changes), Chipotle is safe and satisfying for people with celiac disease.

This guide covers everything that's GF at Chipotle, how to minimize cross-contamination, and why it's one of the smartest choices for gluten-free fast food.

🌯 What's Gluten-Free at Chipotle?

✅ Safe to Order (Naturally GF)

🥙 Build-Your-Own Bowls & Salads (Best Choice!)

Burrito bowls and salads are your safest options because they avoid tortillas entirely. You can customize with any protein, rice, beans, toppings, and salsas.

💡 Pro Tip: Order online or via the app—you can specify "Gluten-Free" and they'll see it in the kitchen.

All Proteins (100% Gluten-Free!):

  • ✅ Chicken (adobo-marinated, grilled)
  • ✅ Steak (carne asada)
  • ✅ Barbacoa (shredded beef)
  • ✅ Carnitas (braised pork)
  • ✅ Sofritas (spiced tofu—vegan & GF)
  • ✅ Smoked Brisket (when available)

Rice & Beans (All GF):

  • ✅ White Cilantro-Lime Rice
  • ✅ Brown Cilantro-Lime Rice
  • ✅ Black Beans
  • ✅ Pinto Beans

Toppings & Extras (All GF):

  • ✅ Fajita Veggies (peppers & onions)
  • ✅ Lettuce
  • ✅ Cheese (shredded Monterey Jack blend)
  • ✅ Sour Cream
  • ✅ Guacamole
  • ✅ Queso Blanco

Salsas (All GF!):

  • ✅ Fresh Tomato Salsa (Mild)
  • ✅ Roasted Chili-Corn Salsa (Medium)
  • ✅ Tomatillo Green-Chili Salsa (Medium)
  • ✅ Tomatillo Red-Chili Salsa (Hot)

Tortillas (Corn Only):

  • ✅ Corn Tortillas (for tacos—naturally GF)
  • ❌ Flour Tortillas (contain gluten—AVOID)
  • ❌ Crispy Taco Shells (fried, may have cross-contamination)

Chips & Extras:

  • ✅ Tortilla Chips (fried corn chips—naturally GF)
  • ✅ Side of Guacamole
  • ✅ Side of Queso

❌ NOT Gluten-Free (Only 3 Items!)

⚠️ Avoid These 3 Items

1. Flour Tortillas — Obviously contain gluten. Order bowls or corn tortillas instead.

2. Crispy Taco Shells — May have cross-contamination in fryers.

3. Chipotle-Honey Vinaigrette — Contains gluten (malt vinegar). Use other salsas instead.

That's it! Only 3 things to avoid out of the entire menu.

⚠️ Cross-Contamination at Chipotle

Chipotle's official position: "We cannot guarantee our foods are completely allergen-free." However, they're better than most fast-food chains at minimizing risk.

Cross-Contamination Risks:

1. Shared Prep Line: Flour tortillas are prepared on the same line as your bowl. Flour dust can settle on toppings bins.

💡 Mitigation: Request glove changes. Staff will change gloves before assembling your order—this is standard protocol.

2. Corn Tortilla Warming: Corn tortillas are warmed on the same surface as flour tortillas.

💡 Mitigation: Request corn tortillas be heated separately or skip warming entirely (they're fine cold in tacos).

3. Shared Serving Spoons: The same spoons scoop ingredients for both flour and corn orders.

💡 Mitigation: Order bowls (not tacos) to minimize tortilla contact. Request fresh ingredients from the back if you're highly sensitive.

📋 How to Order at Chipotle with Celiac Disease

Step-by-step safe ordering:

  1. Order a bowl or salad: "Burrito bowl, please."
  2. Request glove change FIRST: "I have celiac disease. Can you please change gloves?"
  3. Choose your protein: All are safe—pick your favorite
  4. Add rice & beans: Both white and brown rice are GF, as are both beans
  5. Load up toppings: Fajita veggies, all salsas, cheese, sour cream, guac—all safe!
  6. Skip the vinaigrette: If ordering a salad, don't add the chipotle-honey vinaigrette (contains gluten)
  7. Add chips & guac on the side: Both are GF!

🌮 Should You Get Corn Tortilla Tacos?

The tradeoff: Corn tortillas are gluten-free, but they're warmed on the same surface as flour tortillas.

For mild gluten sensitivity: ✅ Go for it! Corn tortilla tacos are delicious.

For celiac disease: ⚠️ Bowls are safer. If you really want tacos, request corn tortillas be warmed separately or ask for cold tortillas on the side.

Honestly? Just get a bowl. Chipotle bowls are massive, satisfying, and eliminate the corn tortilla cross-contamination risk entirely.

💡 Why Chipotle is Great for GF Diners

Chipotle is the best national fast-food chain for celiac disease, and here's why:

✅ Almost Entirely Gluten-Free Menu

Only 3 items contain gluten out of 20+ menu components. Compare that to McDonald's, Wendy's, or Burger King—Chipotle wins by a mile.

✅ Simple, Transparent Ingredients

Chipotle doesn't hide gluten in marinades, sauces, or seasonings. What you see is what you get—grilled meat, rice, beans, veggies.

✅ Staff Training on Allergens

Chipotle trains employees on glove changes and allergen protocols. Not perfect, but better than most fast food.

✅ Customizable & Filling

Build exactly what you want. Bowls are huge and packed with protein—you won't leave hungry.

✅ Online Ordering with Dietary Flags

Order via app and mark "Gluten-Free" so the kitchen sees it before preparing your meal.

🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

Is Chipotle gluten free?

Yes! Chipotle is one of the most gluten-free-friendly fast-food chains. Almost everything is naturally GF including all proteins, rice, beans, salsas, and corn tortillas. Only flour tortillas and crispy shells contain gluten.

What can I eat at Chipotle if I have celiac disease?

Order a burrito bowl with any protein, rice, beans, fajita veggies, and any salsa. Add cheese, sour cream, and guac. Request glove changes. Skip flour tortillas and the vinaigrette salad dressing. This is safe for celiacs with proper precautions.

Are Chipotle bowls gluten free?

Yes! Burrito bowls are completely gluten-free as long as you avoid flour tortillas (obviously not in bowls) and the chipotle-honey vinaigrette.

Are Chipotle chips gluten free?

Yes! Chipotle's tortilla chips are made from corn and are gluten-free. They're fried fresh daily and safe for celiacs.

Are Chipotle corn tortillas gluten free?

Yes. Chipotle's corn tortillas are gluten-free by ingredients. However, they're warmed on the same surface as flour tortillas, so request separate warming or skip warming if you're highly sensitive.

Is Chipotle queso gluten free?

Yes! Chipotle's Queso Blanco is gluten-free. Load it on your bowl or get it as a side for chips.

Is Chipotle guacamole gluten free?

Yes. Chipotle's guacamole is naturally gluten-free (avocados, lime, cilantro, onion, jalapeño, salt).

Can I eat Chipotle with celiac disease?

Yes! Chipotle is one of the safest fast-food options for celiac disease. Request glove changes, order bowls, and avoid flour tortillas. Cross-contamination risk exists but is manageable.

Should I order online or in person at Chipotle?

Online/app ordering is better. You can flag "Gluten-Free" dietary needs, and the kitchen sees it before preparing. Less chance of confusion compared to ordering at the counter during a rush.

💡 Final Verdict

✅ Chipotle is a TOP Choice for GF Fast Food

For celiac disease: ✅ Highly recommended. Request glove changes, order bowls, and you're set. One of the safest fast-food chains.

For mild gluten sensitivity: ✅ Excellent. Order with confidence.

Our take: Chipotle is the best national fast-food chain for gluten-free dining. The menu is 95% naturally GF, portions are generous, and staff understand dietary needs. If you're traveling or need quick GF food, Chipotle is always a solid choice.
