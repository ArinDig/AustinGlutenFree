## DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Brand Colors (Based on your site)
- Primary: Warm earth tones
- Accent: Fresh greens (suggests healthy)
- Background: Clean whites/light grays

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, easy to read
- Body: Clean sans-serif
- Always readable at small mobile sizes

### Image Content
- High-quality food photos
- People enjoying meals (if possible)
- Austin landmarks in background
- Bright, appetizing lighting

### Text Overlay Tips
- Use high contrast (dark text on light bg or vice versa)
- Keep it short: 4-6 words max
- Put key info in center "safe zone" (away from edges)
- Test readability on mobile first
