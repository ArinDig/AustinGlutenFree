## Page Maintenance Tips

1. **Post Frequency:** 3-4 times per week
   - Restaurant features (2x/week)
   - Community engagement (1x/week)  
   - New content/updates (1x/week)

2. **Best Posting Times:** 
   - Weekdays: 11am-1pm (lunch planning) or 5-7pm (dinner planning)
   - Weekends: 9-11am (brunch planning)

3. **Content Mix:**
   - 40% Restaurant features/reviews
   - 30% Helpful tips & guides
   - 20% Community engagement questions
   - 10% Site updates & new listings

4. **Always Include:**
   - Link to website or specific page
   - Relevant hashtags (3-5 per post)
   - High-quality food photos
   - Clear call-to-action
