## A/B Testing Plan

### Week 1-2: Test Ad Creative
- Run all 4 ads simultaneously
- Identify top 2 performers
- Pause bottom 2 performers

### Week 3-4: Test Copy Variations
- Create 2-3 headline variations for top performers
- Test different CTAs
- Optimize based on CTR

### Week 5+: Scale Winners
- Increase budget on best performers
- Expand audience to similar demographics
- Create lookalike audiences from website visitors

---

## Performance Benchmarks

### Success Metrics:
- **CTR (Click-Through Rate):** Target 1-2%+ (good), 2%+ (excellent)
- **CPC (Cost Per Click):** Target $0.50-$1.50 (reasonable for local targeting)
- **CPM (Cost Per 1000 Impressions):** Target $10-20
- **Landing Page Views:** Track in Facebook Pixel
- **Time on Site:** Monitor in Google Analytics

### Red Flags:
- CTR below 0.5% = Creative needs improvement
- CPC above $2 = Audience too broad or creative not resonating
- High CTR but low landing page views = Page load issues

## Budget Optimization Tips

1. **Start Small:** $10/day is enough to gather data
2. **Monitor Daily:** Check performance for first week
3. **Kill Losers Fast:** Pause underperforming ads after 3-4 days
4. **Scale Winners:** Increase budget by 20% every 3-4 days on winners
5. **Retargeting:** After 2 weeks, create retargeting campaign for site visitors

---

## Advanced Strategy (Month 2+)

### Retargeting Campaign
- Target website visitors from past 30 days
- Smaller budget ($3-5/day)
- Different messaging: "Ready to try one of these spots?"
- Typically lower CPC and higher conversion

### Lookalike Audience
- Create 1% lookalike of website visitors
- Expand reach to similar people
- Often performs better than interest-based targeting

### Seasonal Campaigns
- Valentine's Day → Date Night Guide
- Brunch season → Breakfast Guide  
- Pizza specials → Pizza Guide
