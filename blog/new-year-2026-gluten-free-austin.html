üìÖ New Year's Resolutions for GF Diners

Make 2026 your best gluten-free year yet with these resolutions:

1. Try One New Restaurant a Month

With 40+ options in our directory, there's always somewhere new to explore. Set a goal to visit one new GF-friendly spot each month. You might find your new favorite!

2. Support 100% GF Businesses

Dedicated gluten-free restaurants take a risk to serve our community. Show them love! Dine at places like Picnik, Casa de Luz, and Zucchini Kill Bakery regularly.

3. Leave Reviews

Your reviews on Google, Yelp, and Find Me Gluten Free help other celiacs find safe dining. When a restaurant does it right, say so! It also encourages other restaurants to step up their GF game.

4. Ask Questions Without Apology

Your health matters. Don't feel embarrassed asking about cross-contamination, shared fryers, or ingredients. Good restaurants appreciate informed diners.
