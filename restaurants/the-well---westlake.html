About The Well

The Well blends clean ingredients, local and sustainable farming, and authentic flavors for a culinary experience that redefines eating well in Austin, Texas.

They merge culinary expertise with intentional sourcing to create a home for conscious nourishment. Working with local farms, nutritionists, and culinary experts, The Well offers healthy meals for dine-in, pick-up, or delivery.

Dietary Information

The Well is proud to offer recipes that are completely free of:

  • Gluten
  • Dairy
  • Seed Oils
  • Soy
  • Refined Sugar
  • GMOs

They emphasize the importance of low sugar, healthy fats, and clean oils, utilizing only the highest quality ingredients. Any produce on the "Dirty Dozen" list is organically sourced.

Optional add-ons of premium grass-fed dairy are available to enhance your favorite dishes.

Why Choose The Well

The Well takes the guesswork out of eating well, serving up deliciously conscious food no matter your dietary choices. Key features include:

  • Local Sourcing: Partnerships with local farms for fresh ingredients
  • Nutritionist Input: Menus designed with health experts
  • Clean Cooking: No inflammatory oils or processed ingredients
  • Flexible Dining: Dine-in, takeout, or delivery options
  • Multiple Locations: Three convenient Austin locations
