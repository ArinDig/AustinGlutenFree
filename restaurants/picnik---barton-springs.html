About Picnik - Barton Springs

100% gluten-free Modern American cuisine. Known for their bulletproof coffee and healthy, delicious meals. Barton Springs location.

Gluten-Free Information

Picnik Barton Springs is a 100% gluten-free restaurant, making it completely safe for celiacs and those with gluten sensitivities. They use dedicated gluten-free ingredients and preparation methods throughout their kitchen.

  • 100% GF
  • Paleo Options
  • Organic Ingredients

Popular Menu Items

Picnik is famous for their bulletproof coffee, made with grass-fed butter and MCT oil. Their menu features healthy American cuisine including grass-fed burgers, fresh salads, and nutrient-dense bowls. All items are gluten-free and many are paleo-friendly.

Location & Atmosphere

Located on Barton Springs Road near Zilker Park, Picnik Barton Springs offers a casual, health-focused dining experience. The restaurant is popular with fitness enthusiasts and those seeking clean, nutritious meals in Austin.
