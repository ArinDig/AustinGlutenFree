About Flower Child - Domain

Located in the heart of The Domain's Rock Rose district, this Flower Child location brings healthy, feel-good food to North Austin's premier shopping and entertainment destination. Perfect for a nutritious break between shopping, before catching a movie, or meeting friends for a casual healthy meal.

The Domain location benefits from its prime position in one of Austin's most popular outdoor shopping and dining districts. With a bright, welcoming atmosphere and a menu designed to make you feel great, it's become a go-to for health-conscious shoppers and local workers alike.

Part of Fox Restaurant Concepts (the same group behind True Food Kitchen), Flower Child focuses on fresh, organic ingredients and caters to various dietary needs including gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan diners.

Gluten-Free Dining at Flower Child

Flower Child is exceptionally GF-friendly with clearly marked menu items:

  • Clearly Marked Menu: GF items labeled with prominent "GF" indicators
  • Extensive GF Options: More than half the menu is naturally GF or easily modified
  • Customizable Plates: Build your own bowl or plate with GF ingredients
  • Trained Staff: Team knowledgeable about dietary restrictions and cross-contamination
  • App Ordering: Filter for GF options when ordering through their app
  • Catering Available: GF options for events and group orders

Cross-Contamination Note: While they have extensive GF options, this is not a dedicated GF kitchen. Flower Child prepares items with gluten (bread and some sauces). Inform staff of celiac disease for extra precautions‚Äîthey're generally excellent at accommodating.

Popular GF Menu Items

Many of Flower Child's signature items are gluten-free:

  • Mother Earth Bowl (GF): Ancient grains, sweet potato, portobello mushroom, avocado, leafy greens with lemon tahini
  • Glow Bowl (GF): Quinoa, wild-caught salmon, avocado, pickled vegetables
  • Grass-Fed Steak Plate (GF): Premium steak with two GF sides
  • Organic Chicken Plate (GF): Free-range chicken with seasonal vegetables
  • Organic Tofu Plate (GF): Crispy tofu with your choice of sides
  • Thai Cashew Quinoa (GF): Spicy Thai-inspired bowl
  • Superfood Salads (GF): Various creative salads with GF dressings

Tips for GF Ordering at The Domain

Make the most of your Flower Child visit:

  • Check the Menu Key: Look for the GF symbol next to items
  • Order Through the App: Filter by GF and have your order ready for pickup
  • Build Your Own: The build-your-own option lets you control exactly what goes in
  • Ask About Sauces: Most dressings are GF but verify before ordering
  • Go Off-Peak: The lunch rush (12-1pm) can be busy‚Äîgo earlier or later

The Domain Location Experience

The Rock Rose location features Flower Child's signature bright, plant-filled aesthetic with indoor seating and a pleasant patio on the Rock Rose pedestrian walkway. The fast-casual format‚Äîorder at the counter, food brought to your table‚Äîmakes it efficient while still feeling like a sit-down experience.
