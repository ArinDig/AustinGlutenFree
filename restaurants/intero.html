About Intero

Intero brings intimate, soulful Italian cooking to East Austin's Cesar Chavez corridor. This neighborhood gem is known for its handmade pasta, thoughtfully curated wine selection, and‚Äîuniquely‚Äîits own line of handcrafted artisan chocolates that make perfect gifts or a sweet end to your meal.

The restaurant's cozy atmosphere, complete with a charming patio, makes it ideal for romantic dinners, small celebrations, or a leisurely meal with friends. For gluten-free diners, Intero offers pasta substitutions and several naturally GF options.

Gluten-Free Dining at Intero

Intero accommodates gluten-free guests thoughtfully:

  • GF Pasta Available: Gluten-free pasta substitutions available for many dishes
  • Naturally GF Proteins: Grilled meats and fish often naturally gluten-free
  • Seasonal Vegetables: Fresh vegetable preparations typically GF
  • Knowledgeable Staff: Team can guide you through GF-safe options
  • Artisan Chocolates: Many of their handcrafted chocolates are naturally GF

Important: Always inform your server about celiac disease. Request GF pasta specifically and confirm all sauce ingredients.

Artisan Chocolates

One of Intero's unique offerings is their line of handcrafted artisan chocolates:

  • Truffles: Handmade with premium ingredients
  • Chocolate Barks: Seasonal varieties available
  • Gift Boxes: Perfect for holidays and special occasions
  • Pre-Orders: Available online for pickup

Note: Many of their chocolates are naturally gluten-free, but always confirm for specific varieties as some may contain gluten-containing ingredients.

What to Avoid

These items typically contain gluten:

  • Regular pasta: Unless substituted with GF pasta
  • Bread service: Standard bread contains gluten
  • Some desserts: Check with your server for GF options
  • Some sauces: Always confirm ingredients
