Gluten-Free Dining at Intero

Intero accommodates gluten-free guests with genuine care and attention:

  • Quality GF Pasta: Gluten-free pasta substitutions available for most pasta dishes
  • Naturally GF Proteins: Grilled meats, fish, and chops are often naturally gluten-free
  • Seasonal Vegetables: Fresh vegetable preparations are typically GF and delicious
  • GF Antipasti: Many starters are naturally safe‚Äîburrata, carpaccio, vegetable preparations
  • Knowledgeable Staff: Team can guide you through GF-safe options with confidence
  • Artisan Chocolates: Many of their handcrafted chocolates are naturally gluten-free‚Äîperfect for dessert

Cross-Contamination Note: Intero is not a dedicated GF kitchen. Flour is used for handmade pasta, and cross-contamination is possible. Always clearly inform your server about celiac disease, request GF pasta specifically, and confirm all sauce ingredients. The kitchen takes allergies seriously when properly informed.
