About Flower Child

Flower Child is a healthy fast-casual restaurant born from Fox Restaurant Concepts, the same group behind True Food Kitchen. Their mission is simple: serve food that makes you feel good. With a focus on fresh, organic ingredients and a menu that caters to various dietary needs, Flower Child has become a go-to for health-conscious Austinites.

The South Lamar location brings the Flower Child experience to one of Austin's most vibrant neighborhoods, offering a bright, welcoming space perfect for a quick healthy lunch or casual dinner.

Gluten-Free Dining at Flower Child

Flower Child is exceptionally GF-friendly with clearly marked menu items:

  • Clearly Marked Menu: GF items are labeled with a "GF" indicator
  • Extensive GF Options: Many bowls, salads, and proteins are naturally GF
  • Customizable: Build your own plate or bowl with GF ingredients
  • Knowledgeable Staff: Team trained on dietary restrictions
  • App Ordering: Filter for GF options when ordering through their app

Note: While they have extensive GF options, this is not a dedicated GF kitchen. Cross-contamination is possible‚Äîinform staff of celiac disease for extra precautions.

Popular GF Menu Items

Many of Flower Child's signature items are gluten-free:

  • Mother Earth Bowl (GF): Ancient grains, sweet potato, portobello, avocado, leafy greens
  • Glow Bowl (GF): Quinoa, salmon, avocado, pickled vegetables
  • Grass-Fed Steak Plate (GF): With seasonal vegetables
  • Organic Tofu Plate (GF): With your choice of sides
  • Superfood Salads (GF): Various salad options with GF dressings
  • GF Brownie: For dessert lovers!

What to Avoid

These items typically contain gluten:

  • Sandwiches and wraps: Unless specified GF
  • Some dressings: Always confirm with staff
  • Breaded proteins: Any breaded items
  • Some desserts: Unless marked GF
