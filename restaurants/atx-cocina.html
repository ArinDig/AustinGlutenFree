About ATX Cocina

ATX Cocina is a culinary revelation for anyone following a gluten-free lifestyle. This isn't just a restaurant with gluten-free options‚Äîit's a 100% gluten-free AND 100% seed oil free establishment that experiments with the boundaries of Mexican cuisine, taking traditional dishes and elevating them with modern approaches and techniques while maintaining complete celiac safety.

Corn, the basis of Mexican cuisine for thousands of years, is the foundation of their kitchen. They nixtamalize and process their masa entirely in-house, sourcing heirloom varieties of corn from small family farms in Mexico. This ancient process‚Äîsoaking dried corn in an alkaline solution‚Äînot only creates the distinctive flavor and aroma of authentic Mexican masa but also ensures complete gluten-free integrity from grain to table.

For celiacs and those with gluten sensitivities, ATX Cocina represents a rare opportunity: the chance to dine without anxiety, without endless questions, without compromise. Every single item on the menu is safe, making this one of the most celiac-friendly fine dining experiences in Texas.

Why ATX Cocina is a Celiac Paradise

ATX Cocina's commitment to clean eating goes far beyond simply avoiding gluten:

  • 100% Gluten-Free Kitchen: Every dish, every ingredient, every sauce is completely gluten-free‚Äîzero cross-contamination risk
  • Seed Oil Free: No canola, vegetable, soybean, or corn oils‚Äîthey cook with healthier alternatives
  • No Refined Sugars: Natural sweeteners only
  • Certified GF Ingredients: All ingredients verified for gluten-free status
  • Heirloom Corn: Sourced directly from small Mexican farms
  • House-Made Masa: Nixtamalized in-house for authentic flavor and safety

The kitchen's dedication to quality ingredients means you can order absolutely anything on the menu with complete confidence. For newly diagnosed celiacs or those visiting Austin and uncertain about where to eat safely, ATX Cocina is an essential destination.

Popular Menu Highlights

The menu features elevated modern Mexican cuisine with standout dishes including:

  • House-Made Tortillas: The star of the show‚Äînixtamalized heirloom corn masa pressed fresh, served warm with compound butter
  • Tableside Guacamole: Made to order with premium avocados, prepared right at your table
  • Ceviches: Fresh Gulf seafood with bright citrus, chilies, and seasonal accompaniments
  • Enchiladas: Traditional preparation with house-made salsas in rich, complex moles
  • Wood-Fired Proteins: Grilled meats and seafood prepared over open flame
  • Seasonal Specials: Chef's rotating creations featuring the freshest ingredients

The bar program is equally impressive, featuring organic agaves, curated tequilas and mezcals sourced directly from Mexico, craft cocktails made with freshly-squeezed juices, and an excellent wine list. All cocktails are also gluten-free.

  • Brunch Hours: 11 AM - 3 PM every Sunday
  • Chilaquiles: Crispy tortilla chips in salsa with eggs‚Äîa brunch must-try

Location, Hours & Reservations

Hours:

  • Sunday: Brunch 11 AM - 3 PM, Happy Hour 3-5 PM, Dinner 5-9 PM
