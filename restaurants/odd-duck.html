About Odd Duck

Restaurant with separate gluten-free menu and staff conscientious about dietary restrictions. Frequently changing menu based on local ingredients.

Gluten-Free Information

This restaurant offers excellent gluten-free options in Austin. They are known for their commitment to providing safe dining experiences for those with celiac disease and gluten sensitivities.

  • GF Options
  • Celiac Safe
  • Dedicated Prep

Popular Menu Items

Features classic American dishes with gluten-free adaptations including burgers, salads, and comfort food favorites. All items are prepared with gluten-free ingredients and dedicated cooking methods.

Location & Atmosphere

Offers a welcoming American dining experience with gluten-free options clearly marked on the menu. Popular with families and those seeking traditional American cuisine made safe for celiacs.
