About Casa de Luz

Plant-based Mexican cuisine with many naturally gluten-free options. Fresh, organic ingredients and a warm, community atmosphere.

Gluten-Free Information

This restaurant offers excellent gluten-free options in Austin. They are known for their commitment to providing safe dining experiences for those with celiac disease and gluten sensitivities.

  • GF Options
  • Celiac Safe

Popular Menu Items

Provides cafe-style dining with gluten-free options including breakfast items, sandwiches, and light meals. Many items are naturally gluten-free or specially prepared.

Location & Atmosphere

Offers a casual cafe atmosphere with gluten-free options clearly marked. Popular for breakfast, lunch, and light dining with celiac-safe options.
