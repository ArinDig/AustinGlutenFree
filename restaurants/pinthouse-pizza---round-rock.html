About Pinthouse Pizza - Round Rock

Pinthouse Pizza's expansion to Round Rock brought their award-winning combination of craft beer and creative pizza to the growing northern suburbs. Located in the Hoppe Trail development, this location serves families, professionals, and craft beer enthusiasts throughout Williamson County.

The Round Rock Pinthouse maintains the same commitment to quality that made the Austin locations famous. On-site brewing produces the same award-winning beers, and the kitchen delivers the same exceptional pizzas‚Äîincluding their popular gluten-free crust option.

For gluten-free residents of Round Rock and north Austin, this location is a game-changer. No longer do you need to drive into central Austin for quality GF pizza‚ÄîPinthouse Round Rock brings the same ancient grain crust made with sorghum, amaranth, and teff right to your neighborhood.

Gluten-Free Options at Pinthouse

The Round Rock location offers the same excellent GF options as all Pinthouse locations:

  • Ancient Grain GF Crust: Made with sorghum, amaranth, and teff‚Äîavailable for 10" pizzas
  • Cauliflower Crust: Low-carb GF-friendly alternative
  • Vegan Cheese: Available to pair with GF crust for dairy-free needs
  • Plant-Based Sausage: Vegan protein option that's also gluten-free
  • Full Menu Access: Any pizza on the menu can be made on GF crust
  • Italian Chop Salad: A GF standout‚Äîcrisp greens with Italian flavors, highly recommended!
  • Other Salads: Several naturally GF salad options

Cross-Contamination Note: Pinthouse uses shared ovens and prep areas for all pizzas. The GF crust is stored separately and staff are trained on GF requests, but those with celiac disease should be aware of potential cross-contamination. Always communicate the severity of your gluten sensitivity.

What to Avoid

Be mindful of these items when ordering gluten-free:

  • Regular pizza crust: Contains gluten‚Äîalways specify GF crust
  • Most beers: Traditional beer contains gluten from barley/wheat
  • Breadsticks/Garlic Knots: Made with wheat flour
  • Some appetizers: May be breaded‚Äîconfirm with your server
