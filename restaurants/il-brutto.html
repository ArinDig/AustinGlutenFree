About Il Brutto

Il Brutto ("The Ugly" in Italian) brings authentic Italian cooking to East Austin's vibrant 6th Street corridor. Part of the New Waterloo hospitality group, this neighborhood Italian restaurant celebrates rustic Italian cuisine with handmade pasta, wood-fired preparations, and a focus on quality ingredients.

The restaurant's casual-chic atmosphere, complete with a spacious patio, makes it perfect for everything from date nights to group dinners. For gluten-free diners, Il Brutto offers pasta substitutions and several naturally GF options.

Gluten-Free Dining at Il Brutto

Il Brutto accommodates gluten-free guests with care:

  • GF Pasta Available: Gluten-free pasta can be substituted in many dishes
  • Naturally GF Options: Many antipasti and protein dishes are naturally gluten-free
  • Knowledgeable Staff: Servers trained to identify GF-safe options
  • Online Ordering: GF options available for pickup through Toast
  • Celiac Awareness: Kitchen takes allergy requests seriously

Important: Always inform your server about celiac disease. Confirm sauce ingredients and preparation methods with your server.

Menu Highlights for GF Diners

Il Brutto's menu features several options that can be prepared gluten-free:

  • Antipasti: Many starters like burrata, carpaccio, and vegetable preparations are naturally GF
  • Pasta (with GF sub): Request GF pasta for dishes like cacio e pepe, carbonara, or pomodoro
  • Wood-Fired Proteins: Grilled meats and fish prepared on the wood-fire grill
  • Seasonal Vegetables: Fresh vegetable preparations roasted or grilled
  • Salads: Fresh salads with GF dressings

What to Avoid

These items typically contain gluten:

  • Regular pasta: Unless substituted with GF pasta
  • Bread and focaccia: Standard bread contains gluten
  • Breaded items: Any items with breadcrumb coating
  • Some sauces: Always confirm sauce ingredients

The Il Brutto Experience

Located in the heart of East Austin's revitalized 6th Street area, Il Brutto offers a warm, inviting atmosphere with exposed brick, an open kitchen, and a lovely patio perfect for Austin's weather. The restaurant embodies the Italian tradition of gathering around food and conversation.

Il Brutto is part of New Waterloo's portfolio of Austin hospitality concepts, which includes several hotels and restaurants across the city.
