About Il Brutto

Il Brutto ("The Ugly" in Italian) brings authentic, rustic Italian cooking to East Austin's vibrant 6th Street corridor. Part of the acclaimed New Waterloo hospitality group, this neighborhood Italian restaurant celebrates the soul of Italian cuisine‚Äîhandmade pasta, wood-fired preparations, quality ingredients sourced with intention, and the warmth of gathering around a table with good company.

The name "Il Brutto" embraces the Italian concept that beauty lies in imperfection‚Äîthe rustic, the handmade, the honest. This philosophy extends to their cooking: straightforward, ingredient-driven dishes that let quality shine without unnecessary fuss. The result is Italian food that feels both elevated and genuinely comforting.

For gluten-free diners, Il Brutto offers quality pasta substitutions and a menu rich with naturally GF options, making it one of the better Italian dining experiences available for the celiac community in Austin.

Gluten-Free Dining at Il Brutto

Il Brutto accommodates gluten-free guests thoughtfully, understanding that Italian cuisine‚Äîso often centered on pasta and bread‚Äîcan be challenging for celiacs:

  • Quality GF Pasta: Gluten-free pasta can be substituted in most pasta dishes for a small upcharge
  • Naturally GF Antipasti: Many starters‚Äîburrata, carpaccio, vegetable preparations‚Äîare naturally gluten-free
  • Wood-Fired Proteins: Grilled meats and fish prepared on the wood-fire grill are typically GF
  • Knowledgeable Staff: Servers are trained to identify GF-safe options and communicate with the kitchen
  • Online Ordering: GF options available for pickup through Toast‚Äîdietary preferences can be noted
  • Celiac Awareness: Kitchen takes allergy requests seriously when informed

Cross-Contamination Note: Il Brutto is not a dedicated GF kitchen. Flour is used extensively for handmade pasta and bread. While they accommodate GF requests, cross-contamination is possible. Always clearly inform your server about celiac disease (not just preference) so they can take appropriate precautions. Confirm sauce ingredients and preparation methods with your server.

Must-Try Menu Items for GF Diners

Il Brutto's menu features several excellent options for gluten-free guests:

  • Burrata: Creamy, fresh burrata with seasonal accompaniments‚Äîa perfect starter and naturally GF
  • Beef Carpaccio: Thinly sliced raw beef with capers, arugula, and olive oil‚Äîelegant and naturally GF
  • Seasonal Vegetable Antipasti: Roasted or grilled preparations that showcase fresh produce
  • Pasta with GF Substitution: Classic preparations like cacio e pepe, carbonara, pomodoro, or seasonal specials‚Äîconfirm sauce is GF
  • Wood-Fired Steak: Premium cuts prepared over wood flame with simple, quality preparation
  • Grilled Fish: Fresh fish preparations from the wood-fired grill
  • Roasted Chicken: Simple and satisfying when available
  • Contorni (Sides): Seasonal vegetable sides are typically naturally GF

What to Avoid

Be mindful of these items when dining gluten-free at Il Brutto:

  • Regular handmade pasta: Contains gluten‚Äîalways request GF substitution
  • House bread and focaccia: Standard bread service contains gluten
  • Breaded items: Any items with breadcrumb coating or crust
  • Some sauces: Certain sauces may use flour as a thickener‚Äîalways confirm with your server
  • Desserts: Most Italian desserts contain gluten‚Äîask about any GF options
