About Zucchini Kill Bakery

Zucchini Kill isn't just a bakery‚Äîit's a vegan, gluten-free revolution wrapped in punk rock attitude. Named after the legendary Riot Grrrl band Bikini Kill, this East Austin gem proves that dietary restrictions don't mean sacrificing flavor, creativity, or fun.

Founded by a passionate baker who wanted to create indulgent treats that everyone could enjoy, Zucchini Kill has earned a cult following among Austin's vegan, gluten-free, and punk communities. The bakery operates from a fully dedicated gluten-free and vegan facility, making it one of the safest options in the city for celiacs.

Why Zucchini Kill is Celiac-Safe

Zucchini Kill takes allergen safety as seriously as they take flavor:

  • 100% Dedicated GF Facility: No gluten has ever touched their kitchen equipment
  • 100% Vegan: Also free from dairy and eggs‚Äîgreat for multiple allergies
  • Certified GF Ingredients: All flour blends and ingredients are verified gluten-free
  • Small Batch Production: Everything made fresh with meticulous attention to detail
  • Transparent Labeling: All allergens clearly marked on every item

The combination of vegan AND gluten-free means this bakery is safe for an unusually wide range of dietary needs.

Must-Try Menu Items

Zucchini Kill's rotating menu features creative takes on classic treats:

  • Vegan Swiss Roll ($8/slice, $45 whole): Their signature item‚Äîlight sponge cake rolled with creamy filling. Flavors rotate seasonally (chocolate, strawberry, matcha).
  • Twinkie-Style Cakes ($5): A nostalgic treat made vegan and GF. Soft golden cake with cream filling that rivals the original.
  • Cupcakes ($4-5): Moist, flavorful, and piled high with buttercream. Flavors include classic chocolate, vanilla bean, and rotating specials like lavender or pumpkin spice.
  • Cinnamon Rolls ($6): Warm, gooey, and topped with cream cheese frosting. Best enjoyed fresh from the oven.
  • Cookie Sandwiches ($5): Two soft cookies with cream filling. The chocolate chip version is legendary.
  • Seasonal Specials: Look for holiday items like pumpkin pie, yule logs, and Valentine's treats.

Pro tip: Follow @zucchinikill on Instagram for drop announcements and limited-edition flavors that sell out fast.
