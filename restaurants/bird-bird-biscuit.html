About Bird Bird Biscuit

Bird Bird Biscuit has become an Austin institution, known for their creative biscuit sandwiches and genuine commitment to hospitality. With three locations across Austin, they've built a devoted following who line up for their signature creations. The motto? "Let the biscuit bring the joy!"

What makes Bird Bird special for gluten-free diners is that they offer gluten-free biscuits as an option. You can enjoy their creative sandwich fillings on a GF biscuit, giving celiac diners access to the Bird Bird experience.

Gluten-Free Options

Bird Bird Biscuit accommodates GF diners with:

  • GF Biscuits Available: Substitute a gluten-free biscuit on most sandwiches
  • Online Ordering: Select GF biscuit option when ordering online
  • Kiosk Ordering: GF option available when ordering in-store
  • Staff Awareness: Team trained on dietary accommodations

Important Note: While GF biscuits are available, Bird Bird is not a dedicated GF kitchen. Cross-contamination is possible. Those with severe celiac disease should assess their personal comfort level.

How to Order

Bird Bird operates primarily on an online ordering model:

  • Order Online: Place your order ahead for maximum convenience
  • Pickup Time: You'll receive an estimated pickup time with your order
  • Text Notification: You'll get a text when your order is ready
  • Pickup Window: Head to the front pickup window when notified
  • In-Store Kiosk: Available if you can't order online
  • No Phone Orders: Sorry, phone orders are not accepted
