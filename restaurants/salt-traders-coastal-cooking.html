About Salt Traders Coastal Cooking

Salt Traders Coastal Cooking holds a special place in Austin's gluten-free community as one of the city's only 100% dedicated gluten-free seafood restaurants. Every item on the menu‚Äîfrom the signature flat top fish tacos to the creamy New England-style clam chowder‚Äîis prepared in a kitchen completely free of gluten-containing ingredients. For celiacs and those with severe gluten sensitivities, this means zero cross-contamination risk and the rare freedom to order anything without worry.

Founded with the mission of providing Gulf Coast-inspired seafood that everyone can enjoy safely, Salt Traders has become a destination for gluten-free diners visiting Austin from across the country. The restaurant sources sustainable seafood and uses housemade sauces, rubs, and marinades that are all verified gluten-free.

Why Salt Traders is Celiac-Safe

Unlike restaurants that simply offer "gluten-free options," Salt Traders operates as a completely gluten-free facility. Here's what that means for your safety:

  • 100% GF Kitchen: No wheat, barley, or rye enters the building‚Äîever
  • No Shared Fryers: All fried items use dedicated oil that has never touched gluten
  • Housemade Everything: Sauces, breading, and seasonings are made in-house and verified GF
  • Staff Training: Every team member understands celiac disease protocols
  • Certified Ingredients: Suppliers provide gluten-free certifications for key ingredients

We've spoken with multiple celiac community members who dine here regularly without any reactions‚Äîa testament to their rigorous safety standards.

Must-Try Menu Items

With the entire menu safe to order, choosing can be the hardest part. Here are local favorites that keep gluten-free diners coming back:

  • Flat Top Fish Tacos ($16): Crispy beer-battered fish (yes, GF beer batter!) with house slaw, pickled onions, and chipotle crema on corn tortillas. The batter stays incredibly crispy.
  • New England Clam Chowder ($12): Rich, creamy, and loaded with tender clams. Made with a GF roux that rivals any traditional version.
  • Gulf Oysters (market price): Fresh oysters on the half shell‚Äînaturally gluten-free and sourced daily from the Gulf.
  • Shrimp Po'Boy ($18): Crispy fried Gulf shrimp on a GF hoagie roll with remoulade. A true rarity for celiacs who miss po'boys.
  • Grilled Redfish ($28): Simply prepared with lemon butter and fresh herbs. Perfect for those who prefer unbreaded options.

Pro tip: Save room for the Key Lime Pie‚Äîit's gluten-free with a graham-style crust made from GF graham crackers.

Ordering Tips for Celiacs

While the entire menu is safe, here are tips to enhance your experience:

  • Mention celiac when seated: Staff will confirm the kitchen's protocols and flag your order
  • Ask about daily specials: These rotate and are always GF, but ingredients vary
  • Request the allergy binder: Contains detailed ingredient lists if you have other sensitivities (dairy, shellfish, etc.)
  • Try the happy hour: Weekdays 3-6pm offer discounted oysters and drinks‚Äîsame safe kitchen

Location, Atmosphere & When to Visit

Nestled in Austin's vibrant Second Street District downtown, Salt Traders offers a casual coastal vibe with exposed brick, nautical touches, and an open kitchen where you can watch your food being prepared. The patio is dog-friendly and perfect for Austin's mild evenings.

Best times to visit:

  • Weekday lunch (11am-2pm): Shorter waits, full menu available
  • Happy hour (3-6pm weekdays): Great deals on oysters and appetizers
  • Weekend brunch: Features GF biscuits and shrimp & grits

Parking: Street parking can be tricky downtown. We recommend the parking garage on 2nd Street or using a rideshare. The restaurant does not validate parking.
