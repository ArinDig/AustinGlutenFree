About Dia's Market

Dia's Market is a charming East Austin specialty grocery store and deli that has become a beloved neighborhood institution. Offering a curated selection of quality meats, cheeses, prepared foods, and grocery items, Dia's provides a European-style market experience with authentic Austin charm.

What sets Dia's apart is their dedication to quality ingredients and personal service. The owners have built relationships with local purveyors and carefully select every product in the store. For gluten-free diners, Dia's offers GF bread options for their famous deli sandwiches, ensuring everyone can enjoy their exceptional ingredients.

The market has become a gathering spot for East Austin residents who appreciate good food and community connection. It's the kind of place where the staff knows your name and remembers your regular order.

Gluten-Free Options

Dia's Market offers several GF-friendly options:

  • GF Bread Available: Request gluten-free bread for any sandwich
  • Premium Deli Meats: Quality sliced meats are naturally gluten-free
  • Artisan Cheeses: Extensive cheese selection, all naturally GF
  • Prepared Salads: Many fresh salads are GF-friendly (ask staff)
  • Grab-and-Go Items: Check labels on prepared foods for GF options
  • Grocery Section: Curated selection of GF products for home

Cross-Contamination Note: Dia's is a shared kitchen environment. While they're happy to use GF bread and accommodate requests, sandwiches are made on shared surfaces. Inform staff about celiac disease for extra precautions, though those with severe sensitivity should be aware of the shared prep area.

What to Order

  • The Dia's Sandwich (GF bread): Their signature sandwich with premium meats and house-made spreads
  • Custom Deli Sandwiches: Build your own with quality meats, cheeses, and toppings
  • Prepared Salads: Fresh, seasonal salads made daily
  • Cheese Plates: Selection of imported and domestic artisan cheeses
  • Charcuterie: Quality cured meats from trusted producers (verify GF status)
  • Take-Home Items: Olives, pickles, and specialty grocery items

Tips for GF Ordering

Make the most of your Dia's visit with these tips:

  • Request Fresh Gloves: Ask staff to change gloves when making your GF sandwich
  • Ask About Daily Specials: Some specials may be naturally GF or easily modified
  • Check Prepared Foods: Staff can tell you which prepared items are GF
  • Go for Lunch: The sandwich counter is busiest midday‚Äîgo early or late for more attention to GF prep
  • Explore the Grocery: Their curated selection often includes hard-to-find GF items

The Dia's Experience

Walking into Dia's Market feels like stepping into a European deli. The compact space is filled with carefully curated products: wheels of cheese, jars of imported olives, artisan crackers, and a refrigerated case of prepared foods. The deli counter showcases quality meats ready to be sliced for sandwiches or to take home.

The atmosphere is casual and neighborly. Local East Austin residents stop by for their regular lunch orders, picking up a sandwich and a specialty drink before heading back to work. Weekend mornings bring families grabbing breakfast sandwiches and local coffee. It's the kind of place that makes you feel like you've discovered a hidden gem‚Äîeven though it's been a neighborhood staple for years.

Staff members are knowledgeable about their products and happy to make recommendations or accommodate dietary requests. The personal service is part of what makes Dia's special.
