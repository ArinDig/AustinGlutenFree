About Flower Child - Round Rock

Flower Child brings its healthy, feel-good food concept to Round Rock, offering residents north of Austin access to fresh, organic bowls, salads, and plates. This location maintains the same commitment to quality and dietary accommodation that has made Flower Child a favorite across Texas and beyond.

Part of Fox Restaurant Concepts (the same group behind True Food Kitchen), Flower Child was created around the idea that healthy food should taste amazing and make you feel great. The Round Rock location opened to serve the growing suburban communities north of Austin who were previously driving to the Domain or South Lamar for their Flower Child fix.

The restaurant focuses on real, whole ingredients that nourish your body. Everything is made in-house daily, from scratch, using organic and sustainably-sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Gluten-Free Dining at Flower Child

Flower Child is exceptionally GF-friendly with clearly marked menu items:

  • Clearly Marked Menu: GF items are labeled with a prominent "GF" indicator
  • Extensive GF Options: More than half the menu is naturally gluten-free
  • Customizable Plates: Build your own plate or bowl with GF proteins and sides
  • Knowledgeable Staff: Team is trained on dietary restrictions and cross-contamination
  • App Ordering: Filter for GF options when ordering through their app
  • Separate Prep: Staff can use clean utensils and surfaces when requested

Cross-Contamination Note: While they have extensive GF options, this is not a dedicated GF kitchen. Flower Child does prepare items with gluten (like their bread and some sauces). Inform staff of celiac disease for extra precautions‚Äîthey're generally excellent at accommodating.

Popular GF Menu Items

  • Mother Earth Bowl (GF): Ancient grains, sweet potato, portobello mushroom, avocado, leafy greens with lemon tahini dressing
  • Glow Bowl (GF): Quinoa, wild-caught salmon, avocado, pickled vegetables, leafy greens
  • Grass-Fed Steak Plate (GF): Premium grass-fed steak with choice of two GF sides
  • Organic Chicken Plate (GF): Free-range organic chicken with seasonal vegetables
  • Organic Tofu Plate (GF): Crispy organic tofu with your choice of sides
  • Kale Salad (GF): Superfood salad with avocado, seeds, and GF dressing
  • Thai Cashew Quinoa (GF): Spicy Thai-inspired bowl with vegetables and cashews

Tips for GF Ordering

Maximize your Flower Child experience with these tips:

  • Check the Menu Key: Look for the GF symbol next to menu items
  • Tell Your Server: Let them know about your gluten-free needs right away
  • Ask About Sauces: Most dressings are GF, but verify before ordering
  • Build Your Own: The build-your-own plate option lets you control exactly what goes in your meal
  • Order Online: The app lets you filter by GF, making ordering easier
  • Try Family Style: Their shareable portions work great for families with mixed dietary needs

The Round Rock Experience

The Round Rock Flower Child features the brand's signature bright, welcoming atmosphere with natural materials, live plants, and an upbeat vibe. The space is designed to make you feel good before you even eat‚Äîlots of natural light, cheerful colors, and an open kitchen where you can watch your food being prepared.
