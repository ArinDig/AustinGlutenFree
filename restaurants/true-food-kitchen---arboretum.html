About True Food Kitchen

True Food Kitchen was founded on the principles of Dr. Andrew Weil's anti-inflammatory diet, creating a menu that's as delicious as it is nutritious. The restaurant focuses on seasonal, fresh ingredients and offers an extensive array of options for various dietary needs including gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan.

The Arboretum location brings this health-focused dining experience to Northwest Austin, offering a beautiful space with a seasonal menu that changes to highlight the freshest ingredients available.

Gluten-Free Dining at True Food Kitchen

True Food Kitchen is exceptionally GF-friendly:

  • Clearly Marked Menu: GF items labeled with icons on the menu
  • Extensive Selection: Many dishes are naturally GF or easily modified
  • Anti-Inflammatory Focus: Whole foods approach naturally reduces gluten
  • Knowledgeable Staff: Team trained on dietary accommodations
  • Seasonal Menu: GF options change with the seasons for variety

Note: While they have extensive GF options, this is not a dedicated GF kitchen. Always inform your server about celiac disease.

Popular GF Menu Items

  • Ancient Grains Bowl: Quinoa-based with seasonal vegetables
  • Grass-Fed Burger: Available with GF bun or lettuce wrap
  • Sustainable Fish: Grilled with seasonal preparations
  • Seasonal Salads: Fresh salads with GF dressings
  • Edamame Dumplings (GF): Made with rice wrapper
  • Squash Pie: Their signature GF dessert

The Dr. Weil Philosophy

True Food Kitchen's menu is guided by Dr. Andrew Weil's anti-inflammatory food pyramid, emphasizing:

  • Fresh, seasonal produce
  • Whole grains (with GF alternatives available)
  • Healthy fats like olive oil and avocado
  • Lean proteins and sustainable seafood
  • Reduced sugar and processed ingredients
