About True Food Kitchen - Arboretum

True Food Kitchen Arboretum offers health-focused Mediterranean cuisine with extensive gluten-free options. Located in West Austin's Arboretum shopping center with outdoor patio dining.

Gluten-Free Information

This restaurant offers excellent gluten-free options in Austin. They are known for their commitment to providing safe dining experiences for those with celiac disease and gluten sensitivities.
