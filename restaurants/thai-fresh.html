About Thai Fresh

Thai Fresh is a beloved South Austin institution serving authentic Thai cuisine with extensive gluten-free options. What sets them apart is their in-house gluten-free bakery, offering a full range of sweet treats and desserts that are completely safe for those with celiac disease and gluten sensitivities.

Gluten-Free Information

Thai Fresh takes gluten-free dining seriously, with careful preparation practices and dedicated kitchen areas for their gluten-free bakery items. Staff are well-trained in celiac requirements and can guide you through the menu. Their Thai dishes use tamari instead of regular soy sauce, and they clearly mark all gluten-free options on their menu.

  • Dedicated GF Bakery
  • Celiac Aware Staff
  • Tamari Available
  • GF Menu Marking

Popular Menu Items

Thai Fresh is known for their authentic Pad Thai, Green Curry, and Tom Kha soup - all available gluten-free. Their bakery is famous for gluten-free cakes, cookies, and pastries. The vegan ice cream is also a hit. Don't miss their Thai iced tea and fresh spring rolls as starters.

Location & Atmosphere

Located in the heart of the Bouldin neighborhood in South Austin, Thai Fresh offers a casual, welcoming atmosphere with both indoor and outdoor seating.
