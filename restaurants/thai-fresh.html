About Thai Fresh

Thai Fresh is a beloved South Austin institution serving authentic Thai cuisine with extensive gluten-free options. What truly sets them apart is their in-house gluten-free bakery, offering a full range of sweet treats and desserts that are completely safe for those with celiac disease and gluten sensitivities.

Founded by Jam Sanitchat, Thai Fresh has been serving the Bouldin neighborhood since 2008. Jam's commitment to accommodating dietary restrictions comes from a genuine desire to make delicious food accessible to everyone. The gluten-free bakery was added specifically to give celiac diners the rare opportunity to enjoy dessert after their meal.

The restaurant operates as both a caf√©/bakery during the day and a full-service Thai restaurant in the evening, making it a neighborhood gathering spot from morning to night.

Gluten-Free Information

Thai Fresh takes gluten-free dining seriously with multiple accommodations:

  • Dedicated GF Bakery: In-house bakery produces cakes, cookies, and pastries in a dedicated GF area
  • Tamari Available: GF soy sauce (tamari) used for all GF orders
  • Celiac-Trained Staff: Team understands cross-contamination and celiac requirements
  • Menu Marking: GF items clearly marked on the menu
  • Separate Prep: GF dishes prepared with dedicated utensils when requested

Note: While Thai Fresh has excellent GF protocols, this is not an entirely dedicated GF kitchen. The bakery area is separate, but the main kitchen prepares both GF and regular dishes. Always inform your server about celiac disease for the safest experience.

Popular GF Menu Items

  • Pad Thai (GF): Classic rice noodles with choice of protein, made with tamari
  • Green Curry (GF): Aromatic coconut curry with vegetables and your choice of protein
  • Tom Kha Soup (GF): Creamy coconut soup with galangal, lemongrass, and mushrooms
  • Fresh Spring Rolls (GF): Rice paper rolls with vegetables and peanut sauce
  • Papaya Salad (GF): Traditional som tam with fish sauce (verify GF)
  • Basil Fried Rice (GF): Fragrant rice with Thai basil and vegetables

The GF Bakery

Thai Fresh's gluten-free bakery is a rare gem in Austin. They offer:

  • GF Cakes: Full-sized cakes and slices for special occasions
  • GF Cookies: Rotating selection of fresh-baked cookies
  • GF Pastries: Various sweet treats and baked goods
  • Vegan Ice Cream: House-made vegan ice cream (many flavors GF)
  • Custom Orders: They can create custom GF cakes for events

For celiacs who've gone years without safe bakery treats, this is a special place. Save room for dessert!
