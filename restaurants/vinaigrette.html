Gluten-Free Dining at Vinaigrette

Vinaigrette is naturally friendly to gluten-free diners because salads are inherently GF-friendly:

  • Salad-Focused Menu: Most salads are naturally gluten-free or easily modified
  • Fresh Ingredients: Whole foods and fresh produce as the foundation
  • Customizable: Build your own or modify existing salads
  • Dressing Awareness: Staff can identify GF dressings
  • Online Ordering: Order ahead through Toast for easy pickup

Important: Always confirm with staff about specific ingredients and dressings. Some items may contain hidden gluten in toppings or sauces.
