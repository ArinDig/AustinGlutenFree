What makes The Steeping Room special for gluten-free diners is their commitment to providing GF options for their signature afternoon tea experience, including house-made gluten-free scones.

Gluten-Free Options

The Steeping Room accommodates GF diners with:

  • GF Scones: House-made gluten-free scones available
  • GF Afternoon Tea: Request the GF version of their afternoon tea service
  • GF Bread: Available for sandwiches upon request
  • Naturally GF Teas: All pure teas are naturally gluten-free
  • Salads: Many salad options are GF-friendly

Important: Request GF options when making reservations for afternoon tea, as they may need to prepare items in advance.

The Afternoon Tea Experience

The Steeping Room's signature offering is their afternoon tea service:

  • Traditional Afternoon Tea: Three-tiered tray with scones, finger sandwiches, and sweets
  • GF Afternoon Tea: Same elegant experience with GF scones and bread
  • Premium Teas: Choose from their extensive selection of loose-leaf teas
  • Special Occasions: Perfect for birthdays, bridal showers, or special outings

Tip: Reservations are recommended, especially for weekend afternoon tea. Mention GF needs when booking.

Tea Selection

The Steeping Room features an impressive collection of teas:

  • Black Teas: Classic English Breakfast, Earl Grey, and more
  • Green Teas: Japanese and Chinese varieties
  • Herbal Infusions: Caffeine-free options (naturally GF)
  • Specialty Blends: House-created unique blends

All pure teas are naturally gluten-free. Check flavored blends for any additives.
