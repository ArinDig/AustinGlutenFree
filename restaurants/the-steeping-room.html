Gluten-Free Dining at The Steeping Room

The Steeping Room has developed a thoughtful approach to accommodating gluten-free guests, making it possible to enjoy the full afternoon tea experience with appropriate modifications:

  • House-Made GF Scones: Their gluten-free scones are baked in-house and served warm with clotted cream and jam‚Äîa rare treat for celiac diners
  • GF Afternoon Tea Service: Request the gluten-free version of their signature three-tiered afternoon tea when booking
  • GF Bread for Sandwiches: Available upon request for their tea sandwiches
  • Naturally GF Teas: All pure, unflavored teas are naturally gluten-free
  • GF-Friendly Salads: Many salad options can be prepared gluten-free
  • Staff Awareness: Team trained on dietary accommodations and cross-contamination concerns

Important Note: This is not a dedicated gluten-free kitchen. While they take precautions, cross-contamination is possible. Always inform your server about celiac disease. For afternoon tea, request GF options when making your reservation so they can prepare items in advance.

The Afternoon Tea Experience

The Steeping Room's signature offering is their traditional afternoon tea service, an elegant experience that transports you to a classic British tea room:

  • Traditional Afternoon Tea: The iconic three-tiered tray featuring fresh scones with clotted cream and jam, delicate finger sandwiches with creative fillings, and a selection of house-made sweets and pastries
  • Gluten-Free Afternoon Tea: The same elegant experience thoughtfully adapted with GF scones and bread, allowing celiac diners to participate fully in this classic tradition
  • Premium Tea Selection: Choose from their extensive collection of over 100 loose-leaf teas from around the world
  • Special Occasions: Perfect for birthdays, bridal showers, baby showers, mother-daughter dates, or any celebration deserving of a touch of elegance
  • Seasonal Variations: The menu changes seasonally, featuring fresh, local ingredients and creative flavor combinations

Pro Tip: Reservations are highly recommended for afternoon tea, especially on weekends. When booking, clearly mention your gluten-free requirements so the kitchen can prepare your items separately. Arrive with time to browse their tea selection‚Äîchoosing your tea is part of the experience.

Tea Selection & Expertise

The Steeping Room takes tea seriously, offering an impressive and carefully curated collection:

  • Black Teas: Classic English Breakfast, rich Assam, smoky Lapsang Souchong, fragrant Earl Grey, and seasonal blends
  • Green Teas: Japanese Sencha, Chinese Dragon Well, Gyokuro, and delicate jasmine varieties
  • Oolong Teas: Traditional Taiwanese varieties with complex flavor profiles
  • White Teas: Subtle and elegant options including Silver Needle
  • Herbal Infusions: Caffeine-free options including rooibos, chamomile, peppermint, and unique house blends (all naturally GF)
  • Specialty & Seasonal Blends: Unique house-created blends that change with the seasons

All pure, unflavored teas are naturally gluten-free. For flavored blends, always ask about ingredients as some may contain barley malt or other additives. Their knowledgeable staff can guide you to safe options.
