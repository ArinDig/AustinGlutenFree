About Bouldin Creek Cafe

Bouldin Creek Cafe is an Austin institution‚Äîa beloved vegetarian cafe that has been a cornerstone of the South First Street community since 1997. More than just a restaurant, it's a gathering place where local art adorns the walls, live music fills the air, and everyone from artists to families feels welcome.

The cafe serves scratch-made vegetarian and vegan cuisine with extensive gluten-free options. Their commitment to accommodating dietary restrictions, combined with their community-focused atmosphere, makes it a favorite among Austin's health-conscious diners.

Gluten-Free Dining at Bouldin Creek

Bouldin Creek Cafe takes gluten-free dining seriously:

  • Extensive GF Menu: Many items clearly marked as gluten-free on the menu
  • GF Substitutions: GF bread and tortillas available for many items
  • Naturally GF Options: Many dishes are inherently gluten-free
  • Breakfast All Day: GF breakfast options available throughout service
  • Vegan-GF Overlap: Strong selection for those with multiple dietary needs

Important: While they have extensive GF options, this is not a dedicated GF kitchen. Always inform your server about celiac disease for proper precautions.

Popular GF Menu Items

Bouldin Creek offers many GF-friendly options:

  • Breakfast Tacos: Available with GF tortillas
  • Tofu Scrambles: Made with fresh vegetables and seasonings
  • Bowls: Rice-based bowls with various protein and vegetable options
  • Salads: Fresh salads with house-made dressings (confirm GF status)
  • Smoothies: Naturally gluten-free fresh fruit smoothies
  • Huevos Rancheros: Available GF with corn tortillas

What to Avoid

These items typically contain gluten:

  • Standard bread and toast: Request GF bread if available
  • Flour tortillas: Request corn or GF tortillas instead
  • Baked goods: Unless specifically GF
  • Some sauces and dressings: Always confirm with your server

The Bouldin Creek Experience

What makes Bouldin Creek special goes beyond the food:

  • Local Art: Rotating exhibitions from Austin artists on the walls
  • Live Music: Regular performances from local musicians
  • Community Hub: A gathering place for the South Austin neighborhood
  • Patio Seating: Lovely outdoor space for Austin's great weather
  • Happy Hour: Check their specials page for current offerings

The cafe has a casual, welcoming vibe that encourages lingering over coffee and conversation. It's the kind of place where you might come for breakfast and stay for lunch.

Catering Available

Bouldin Creek offers catering services for events, with many GF-friendly options available. Contact them directly for catering menus and ordering information.
