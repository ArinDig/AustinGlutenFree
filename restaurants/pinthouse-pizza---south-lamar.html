About Pinthouse Pizza - South Lamar

The South Lamar location of Pinthouse Pizza brings the same award-winning combination of craft beer and creative pizza to South Austin. Nestled in the heart of the South Lamar corridor, this location has become a neighborhood favorite for families, date nights, and groups of friends looking for quality food and exceptional brews.

Like its sister locations, the South Lamar Pinthouse features an on-site brewery producing world-class beers that have earned recognition at major competitions. The space is modern and welcoming, with plenty of seating for groups and a lively atmosphere that captures Austin's casual dining culture.

For the gluten-free community, the South Lamar location offers the same excellent ancient grain crust made with sorghum, amaranth, and teff. Reviews consistently praise the taste and texture of this crust‚Äîmany saying it rivals traditional pizza.

Gluten-Free Options at Pinthouse

Pinthouse provides excellent options for gluten-free diners:

  • Ancient Grain GF Crust: Made with sorghum, amaranth, and teff‚Äîavailable for 10" pizzas
  • Cauliflower Crust: An additional GF-friendly option for low-carb preferences
  • Vegan Cheese: Available to pair with GF crust for dairy-free diners
  • Plant-Based Sausage: Vegan protein option that's also gluten-free
  • Full Menu Access: Any pizza on the menu can be made on GF crust
  • Italian Chop Salad: A GF standout‚Äîcrisp greens with Italian flavors, highly recommended!
  • Other Salads: Several naturally GF salad options available

Cross-Contamination Note: Pinthouse uses shared ovens and prep areas for all pizzas. The GF crust is stored separately and staff are trained on GF requests, but those with celiac disease should be aware of potential cross-contamination. Always communicate the severity of your gluten sensitivity to your server.

What to Avoid

Keep these items off your order when eating gluten-free:

  • Regular pizza crust: Contains gluten‚Äîalways specify GF crust
  • Most beers: Traditional beer contains gluten from barley/wheat
  • Breadsticks/Garlic Knots: Made with wheat flour
  • Certain appetizers: Some may be breaded‚Äîconfirm with your server

The South Lamar Location

The South Lamar Pinthouse is perfectly positioned in one of Austin's most dynamic neighborhoods. The area has seen tremendous growth, and Pinthouse fits right in with its modern-industrial aesthetic, communal tables, and family-friendly atmosphere.

This location tends to be slightly less crowded than the original Burnet Road spot, making it a great option if you want a more relaxed dining experience. The parking is easier too, which is always a plus in Austin. Whether you're catching a game on TV, hosting a casual meetup, or just grabbing dinner with the family, South Lamar delivers.
