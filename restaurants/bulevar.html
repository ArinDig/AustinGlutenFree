About Bulevar

Bulevar Mexican Kitchen celebrates the simple pleasures of premium seafood, prime steaks, organic chicken and heirloom vegetables cooked over live coals and shared around the family table. It's a place where fire meets flavor, and where every single dish is 100% gluten-free.

As the sister restaurant to the acclaimed ATX Cocina, Bulevar continues the tradition of excellence in gluten-free dining. The team utilizes modern cooking approaches combined with traditional Latin American flavors and practices, all in a dedicated gluten-free kitchen.

The atmosphere is sophisticated yet welcoming‚Äîfire-lit with a high-energy bar, tall ceilings, and big glass windows that draw you to the patio for stunning Austin views and sunsets. It's the kind of place that feels special without being stuffy.

Why Bulevar is Perfect for Celiacs

Bulevar operates a 100% gluten-free kitchen, making it a true sanctuary for those with celiac disease and gluten sensitivities:

  • 100% GF Kitchen: No gluten ingredients ever enter the building
  • Celiac Safe: Zero risk of cross-contamination
  • Order Anything: Every single menu item is safe
  • No Questions Needed: Just order and enjoy without anxiety
  • ATX Cocina Heritage: Same commitment to GF excellence as their sister restaurant

For celiacs who've spent years worrying about restaurant meals, Bulevar offers something precious: a genuinely upscale dining experience with complete peace of mind.

Signature Menu Items

The menu features wood-fired Mexican cuisine with standout dishes including:

  • Wood-Fired Steaks: Premium cuts cooked over live coals with Mexican seasonings
  • Grilled Seafood: Fresh catches prepared with bold Mexican spices and citrus
  • Organic Chicken: Free-range poultry with traditional herb rubs
  • Heirloom Vegetables: Seasonal produce from local farms, charred to perfection
  • House-Made Tortillas: Corn tortillas made fresh (naturally GF)
  • Tacos and Ceviches: Light bites perfect for sharing
  • GF Desserts: Complete your meal with safe sweet options

The Bar at Bulevar

The high-energy bar is a destination in itself:

  • Craft Margaritas: House-made with fresh citrus and quality tequila
  • Mezcal Selection: Extensive collection of artisanal mezcals
  • Happy Hour: Daily drink specials (Mon-Thu 5-6pm, Fri-Sun 4-6pm)
  • GF-Safe Cocktails: All cocktails are made with GF ingredients

Located in the Arboretum area of North Austin, Bulevar offers a sophisticated dining atmosphere that feels celebratory. The design emphasizes the open kitchen and wood-fired grill‚Äîyou can watch your meal being prepared over live coals.
