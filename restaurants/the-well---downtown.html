The Well Philosophy

The Well takes the guesswork out of eating well, serving up deliciously conscious food no matter your dietary choices. Key features include:

  • Local Sourcing: Partnerships with local farms for the freshest ingredients
  • Nutritionist Input: Menus designed in collaboration with health experts
  • Clean Cooking: No inflammatory oils or processed ingredients ever
  • Organic Produce: Any produce on the "Dirty Dozen" list is organically sourced
  • Flexible Dining: Dine-in, takeout, or delivery options

They emphasize the importance of low sugar, healthy fats, and clean oils, utilizing only the highest quality ingredients. Optional add-ons of premium grass-fed dairy are available for those who tolerate it.

The Downtown Experience

The Downtown location on 2nd Street offers a modern, health-focused atmosphere in the heart of Austin. The space is bright and airy, with clean lines and natural materials that reflect the brand's wellness philosophy.

This location is perfect for lunch meetings, dinner dates, or a healthy meal any time. The downtown setting attracts a mix of business professionals, health-conscious diners, and celiac community members who've finally found a restaurant where they can eat with complete confidence.

Parking downtown can be challenging, but the restaurant validates for nearby garages. Consider delivery or pickup during peak hours.
