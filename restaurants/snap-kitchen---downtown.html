About Snap Kitchen

Snap Kitchen revolutionized healthy eating in Austin with its grab-and-go concept of pre-made, nutritionist-designed meals. Founded in Austin, Snap Kitchen has become a go-to for busy professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone seeking convenient, healthy food options.

The Downtown location on Congress Avenue serves the Capitol area and downtown workers with quick access to refrigerated cases full of ready-to-eat meals, perfect for those who need healthy food fast without sacrificing nutritional quality.

Gluten-Free Dining at Snap Kitchen

Snap Kitchen makes GF eating easy:

  • Clearly Labeled: Every meal is labeled with dietary information including GF status
  • Extensive Selection: Many meals are naturally gluten-free or designed to be GF
  • Nutritional Info: Full ingredient lists and nutritional information on every package
  • Keto/Paleo Options: Many of these specialty meals are also GF
  • Online Ordering: Filter by dietary preference when ordering online

Note: While meals are prepared with care, they are made in a shared kitchen. Those with severe allergies should review ingredient lists carefully.

How It Works

  • Walk In: Browse refrigerated cases of pre-made meals
  • Check Labels: Each meal clearly shows GF, keto, paleo, and other dietary markers
  • Grab and Go: Purchase and eat immediately or heat at home/office
  • Order Online: Order ahead for pickup or delivery
  • Meal Plans: Subscribe for regular healthy meal delivery

Popular GF Meal Categories

  • Protein Bowls: Grilled proteins with vegetables and grains
  • Breakfast: Egg-based dishes, overnight oats (verify GF)
  • Salads: Fresh salads with house-made dressings
  • Keto Meals: Low-carb, often naturally GF
  • Paleo Meals: Grain-free and typically GF
