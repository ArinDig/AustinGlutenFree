About Lil'Doddy Burger Lab

Lil'Doddy Burger Lab is Hopdoddy Burger Bar's fast-casual concept, bringing the same quality fresh beef and craft burgers to a quicker, more convenient format. With counter service and a drive-thru option, Lil'Doddy is perfect for when you want a quality burger without the wait of a full-service restaurant.

Like its parent restaurant, Lil'Doddy offers gluten-free buns as a substitution option, making it a great choice for GF diners seeking a satisfying burger experience.

Gluten-Free Options

Lil'Doddy accommodates GF diners with the following:

  • GF Buns Available: Substitute a gluten-free bun on any burger
  • Lettuce Wrap Option: Skip the bun entirely for a naturally GF option
  • Fresh-Ground Beef: Quality beef that's naturally gluten-free
  • Salad Options: Burger salads with GF dressings

Important: While GF buns are available, this is a shared kitchen with wheat products. Those with severe celiac should be aware of potential cross-contamination from shared grills and prep surfaces.

What to Order

  • Classic Burger + GF Bun: Fresh-ground beef, lettuce, tomato, pickles
  • Any Signature Burger + GF Bun: All burgers can be made with GF bun
  • Lettuce-Wrapped Burger: Skip the bun for a naturally GF option
  • Salads: Check for GF dressing options

What to Avoid

  • Regular buns: Contain gluten
  • Some sauces: May contain wheat-based thickeners‚Äîconfirm with staff
  • Breaded items: Any breaded toppings or sides

The Lil'Doddy Experience

Unlike the sit-down Hopdoddy Burger Bar experience, Lil'Doddy offers quick counter service and a convenient drive-thru. Order at the counter, grab your number, and your fresh-made burger will be brought to you. The drive-thru makes it especially convenient for a quick GF meal on the go.
