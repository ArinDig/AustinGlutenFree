About Lil'Doddy Burger Lab

Lil'Doddy Burger Lab is Hopdoddy Burger Bar's fast-casual concept, bringing the same quality fresh-ground beef and craft burger expertise to a quicker, more convenient format. With counter service and a drive-thru option, Lil'Doddy is perfect for when you want a premium burger without the wait (and sometimes lengthy lines) of the full-service Hopdoddy experience.

The "Lab" concept allows Hopdoddy to experiment with new menu items and formats while maintaining their commitment to quality. Fresh beef ground daily, scratch-made sauces, and attention to detail‚Äîall in a fraction of the time.

Like its parent restaurant, Lil'Doddy offers gluten-free buns as a substitution option, making it one of the few fast-casual burger joints where GF diners can get a real craft burger experience.

Gluten-Free Options

Lil'Doddy accommodates GF diners with the following:

  • GF Buns Available: Substitute a quality gluten-free bun on any burger
  • Lettuce Wrap Option: Skip the bun entirely for a naturally GF option
  • Fresh-Ground Beef: Quality beef that's naturally gluten-free
  • Salad Options: Burger bowls and salads available
  • Shake Options: Many shakes are naturally GF (confirm with staff)

Cross-Contamination Note: While GF buns are available, this is a shared kitchen with wheat products. Burgers are cooked on shared grills and prep surfaces. Those with severe celiac should be aware of potential cross-contamination‚Äîthis is best for GF diners who can tolerate trace exposure.

What to Order

  • Classic Burger + GF Bun: Fresh-ground beef, lettuce, tomato, pickles, house sauce
  • Signature Burgers + GF Bun: All specialty burgers can be made with GF bun
  • Lettuce-Wrapped Burger: Protein-style for the most GF-friendly option
  • Burger Bowl: All the toppings over greens without the bun
  • Shakes: Premium milkshakes (confirm GF status of specific flavors)

What to Avoid

  • Regular buns: Standard buns contain gluten
  • Some sauces: A few specialty sauces may contain wheat-based thickeners
  • Breaded items: Any breaded toppings like fried onion strings
  • Fries (potentially): Ask about shared fryer situation

Tips for Ordering GF

Get the best experience at Lil'Doddy:

  • Say "GF bun" clearly: Make sure they note it on your order
  • Ask about sauces: Confirm which house sauces are GF
  • Consider lettuce wrap: Most reliable option for severe sensitivity
  • Use the drive-thru: Same quality, super convenient
  • Go off-peak: Kitchen has more attention for special requests during slower times

The Lil'Doddy Experience

Unlike the sit-down Hopdoddy Burger Bar experience with its full bar and longer waits, Lil'Doddy is built for speed and convenience. Order at the counter, grab your number, and your fresh-made burger arrives quickly at your table. The drive-thru option is a game-changer for GF diners who want a quality burger without leaving their car.

The Oak Hill location on William Cannon serves the southwest Austin area, providing an accessible option for families and commuters in the area. The atmosphere is casual and modern‚Äîperfect for a quick lunch, dinner on the go, or feeding hungry kids after practice.

Best of all, you're getting Hopdoddy quality without the South Congress lines and wait times. For GF diners who've been missing good craft burgers, Lil'Doddy fills that void nicely.
