About Bougie's Donuts & Coffee

Gluten-free donut shop and coffee house in Sunset Valley. Specializes in artisanal gluten-free donuts and premium coffee.

Gluten-Free Information

Bougie's Donuts & Coffee is a 100% gluten-free establishment, making it completely safe for those with celiac disease and gluten sensitivities. All donuts are made in a dedicated gluten-free facility with certified gluten-free ingredients, ensuring no cross-contamination risk.

  • 100% GF
  • Celiac Safe
  • Dedicated Prep

Popular Menu Items

Specializes in artisanal gluten-free donuts with creative flavors and premium coffee. All items are made fresh daily in a dedicated gluten-free facility. The menu features classic donut varieties as well as seasonal specials, all completely safe for celiacs.

Location & Atmosphere

Located in Sunset Valley, Bougie's provides a welcoming coffee shop atmosphere perfect for enjoying gluten-free donuts and premium coffee. The space is designed for both grab-and-go and sit-down enjoyment, making it a great spot for breakfast, brunch, or an afternoon treat.
