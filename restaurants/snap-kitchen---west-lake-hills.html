About Snap Kitchen - West Lake Hills

Snap Kitchen serves the West Lake Hills community with its popular healthy grab-and-go concept. Offering nutritionist-designed, pre-made meals that are ready to eat or heat, this location is perfect for busy West Austin residents seeking healthy food options without sacrificing convenience.

The West Lake Hills location caters to an affluent, health-conscious clientele who value quality ingredients and transparent nutrition information. Whether you're a busy professional, a parent managing kids' schedules, or a fitness enthusiast tracking macros, Snap Kitchen provides restaurant-quality meals you can eat at home in minutes.

Every meal comes with full nutritional information and dietary labels, making it easy to find gluten-free options at a glance. The refrigerated cases are thoughtfully organized by dietary preference, so GF shoppers can quickly locate safe options.

Gluten-Free Dining at Snap Kitchen

Snap Kitchen makes GF eating easy with their transparent labeling:

  • Clearly Labeled: Every meal displays dietary information including GF status with easy-to-spot icons
  • Extensive Selection: Typically 15-20 gluten-free options available daily
  • Nutritional Info: Full ingredient lists, macros, calories, and allergen information on every package
  • Keto/Paleo Options: Many specialty diet meals are naturally grain-free and GF
  • Online Ordering: Filter by dietary preference when ordering through their app or website
  • Meal Subscriptions: Set up weekly deliveries with GF-only preferences

Cross-Contamination Note: Snap Kitchen prepares all meals in a central kitchen that also handles gluten-containing ingredients. Meals are packaged separately, but those with severe celiac disease should be aware of this shared facility arrangement.

Popular GF Meal Categories

  • Protein Bowls: Grilled salmon, chicken, or grass-fed beef with seasonal vegetables and cauliflower rice
  • Breakfast: Egg white frittatas, turkey sausage scrambles, and protein-packed egg cups
  • Fresh Salads: Southwest chicken, Asian sesame, Greek, and seasonal specialty salads
  • Keto Meals: Bacon-wrapped proteins, bunless burger bowls, and low-carb vegetable dishes
  • Paleo Meals: Grain-free options with whole proteins and roasted vegetables
  • Healthy Snacks: Hard-boiled eggs, veggie cups with hummus, and protein bites
