About Postino Wine Cafe

Postino Wine Cafe has become an Austin institution for its excellent wine selection, creative bruschetta boards, and effortlessly relaxed atmosphere. Originally founded in Arizona, Postino has expanded across Texas and beyond, bringing their signature concept of affordable wine paired with elevated casual dining to the heart of South Lamar.

The concept is brilliantly simple: exceptional wines at accessible prices, creative bruschetta combinations, and an atmosphere that encourages gathering with friends. The South Lamar location has become a neighborhood favorite, drawing crowds for their legendary $5 wine until 5pm deal and staying busy through evening as a prime spot for casual dates and friend groups alike.

What makes Postino special for gluten-free diners is their genuine willingness to accommodate with high-quality gluten-free bread options for their famous bruschetta and paninis. While this isn't a dedicated GF kitchen, their staff is well-trained on accommodating dietary needs, making it possible for celiac diners to participate in the Postino experience.

Gluten-Free Dining at Postino

Postino offers several excellent ways for GF diners to enjoy their full menu experience:

  • GF Bread Available: Quality gluten-free bread can be substituted on any bruschetta or panini order
  • Fresh Salads: Several salad options featuring fresh ingredients with GF-friendly dressings (confirm ingredients with server)
  • Cheese & Charcuterie Boards: Naturally gluten-free selections (simply skip the crackers or ask if GF crackers are available)
  • Wine Selection: Their extensive, carefully curated wine list is entirely naturally gluten-free
  • Staff Training: Team members are trained to help identify safe options and communicate with the kitchen
  • Allergy Communication: Kitchen takes allergy requests seriously when informed

Cross-Contamination Note: Postino operates a shared kitchen where gluten-containing items are prepared. While they accommodate GF requests, cross-contamination is possible. Always inform your server about celiac disease so they can take extra precautions, such as using clean prep surfaces and changing gloves.

What to Avoid

Be mindful of these items when dining gluten-free at Postino:

  • Regular bread and focaccia: Contains gluten‚Äîalways request GF substitution
  • Some sauces and dressings: May contain wheat flour as a thickener‚Äîconfirm with your server
