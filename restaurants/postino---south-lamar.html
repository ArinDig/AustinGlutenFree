About Postino

Postino Wine Cafe has become an Austin favorite for its excellent wine selection, creative bruschetta boards, and relaxed atmosphere. Originally from Arizona, Postino has expanded across Texas and beyond, bringing their signature concept of affordable wine and elevated casual dining to South Lamar.

What makes Postino special for gluten-free diners is their willingness to accommodate with gluten-free bread options for their famous bruschetta and paninis.

Gluten-Free Options

Postino offers several ways for GF diners to enjoy their menu:

  • GF Bread Available: Substitute GF bread on bruschetta and paninis
  • Salads: Several salad options with GF-friendly ingredients
  • Cheese & Charcuterie: Naturally GF (skip the crackers)
  • Wine Selection: Extensive wine list (all naturally GF!)
  • Staff Knowledge: Team can help identify GF options

Important: While GF bread is available, this is a shared kitchen. Cross-contamination is possible‚Äîinform your server about celiac disease.

Popular GF-Friendly Items

  • Bruschetta Board (GF bread): Choose your favorite toppings on GF bread
  • Paninis (GF bread): Request any panini with GF bread substitution
  • Salads: Fresh salads with house-made dressings (confirm GF)
  • Cheese Boards: Selection of cheeses (skip crackers for GF)

$5 Wine Special

Postino is famous for their daily wine special:

  • $5 Wine: All wines are $5 until 5pm daily
  • Wine Flights: Try multiple wines in a tasting flight
  • All GF: Wine is naturally gluten-free!

What to Avoid

  • Regular bread: Contains gluten‚Äîrequest GF substitution
  • Some sauces: May contain wheat‚Äîconfirm with server
  • Crackers: Skip the crackers on cheese boards

The Postino Experience

The South Lamar location features a spacious patio perfect for Austin's weather, industrial-chic interior design, and a lively atmosphere that's great for happy hour, dinner with friends, or a casual date night. The wine bar feel makes it perfect for lingering over a glass and some bruschetta.
