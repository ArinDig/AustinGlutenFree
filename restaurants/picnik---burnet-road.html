About Picnik - Burnet Road

Picnik is one of Austin's most beloved 100% gluten-free restaurants, founded on the principles of clean eating, paleo nutrition, and functional foods. The Burnet Road location (also known as the Lamar location, at 6607 N Lamar Blvd) is the original Picnik, where founder Naomi Seifter built a loyal following with her butter coffee and wholesome, grain-free meals.

For celiac diners, Picnik represents the gold standard: a completely gluten-free kitchen where you can order anything on the menu without worry. No cross-contamination, no need to ask questions‚Äîjust safe, delicious food. This is what dining out should feel like for everyone.

Picnik's philosophy goes beyond just avoiding gluten. They focus on nutrient-dense, anti-inflammatory foods that actually make you feel good. Everything is made from scratch with high-quality ingredients: grass-fed meats, organic vegetables, healthy fats, and no refined sugars or seed oils.

The Picnik Experience

The Burnet/Lamar location has a casual, health-focused vibe with counter service. The space is bright and welcoming, decorated with natural materials and plants that reflect the brand's wellness philosophy. You'll see a mix of remote workers on laptops, fitness enthusiasts grabbing post-workout meals, and families enjoying a healthy brunch.

What makes Picnik special beyond the food is the community. Many celiac diners consider Picnik their "safe space"‚Äîa place where they can eat without fear for the first time in years. The staff understands the importance of what they're providing, and that care shows in everything they do.

Pro tip: Parking can be tight during peak hours (weekend brunch especially). Consider going slightly off-peak or using the app for pickup.
