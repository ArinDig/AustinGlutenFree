About Picnik - Burnet Road

Picnik is one of Austin's most beloved 100% gluten-free restaurants, founded on the principles of clean eating, paleo nutrition, and functional foods. The Burnet Road location is the original Picnik, where founder Naomi Seifter built a loyal following with her butter coffee and wholesome, grain-free meals.

For celiac diners, Picnik represents the gold standard: a completely gluten-free kitchen where you can order anything on the menu without worry. No cross-contamination, no need to ask questions‚Äîjust safe, delicious food.
