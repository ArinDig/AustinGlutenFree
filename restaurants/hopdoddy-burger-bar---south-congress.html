About Hopdoddy

Hopdoddy Burger Bar has become an Austin institution since opening on South Congress in 2010. Known for their fresh-ground-daily beef, creative burger combinations, hand-cut fries, and craft cocktails, Hopdoddy set a new standard for the gourmet burger experience in Austin.

The South Congress location is the original Hopdoddy, and its lines out the door have become as iconic as the street itself. For gluten-free diners, Hopdoddy offers GF bun substitutions, making their quality burgers accessible to the celiac community.

Gluten-Free Options

Hopdoddy accommodates GF diners:

  • GF Buns Available: Substitute a gluten-free bun on any burger
  • Lettuce Wrap: Get any burger as a lettuce wrap (naturally GF)
  • Fresh Beef: All beef is naturally gluten-free
  • Salads: Burger salad options available
  • Shakes: Many shake flavors are naturally GF (confirm with staff)

Important: While GF buns are available, burgers are cooked on shared grills. Those with severe celiac should be aware of potential cross-contamination.

Popular Burgers (Order with GF Bun)

  • Classic Burger: Fresh beef, lettuce, tomato, pickles, H-sauce
  • Goodnight/Good Cause: Beef, bacon, cheddar, bbq sauce, fried onion (skip onion for GF)
  • Magic Shroom: Beef, swiss, truffle aioli, saut√©ed mushrooms
  • Greek Burger: Lamb & beef blend with feta, tzatziki
  • Veggie Burger: Black bean patty (confirm GF status)

What to Avoid

  • Regular buns: Contain gluten
  • Fried onion strings: May be breaded
  • Some sauces: Ask about specific ingredients
  • Breaded items: Any breaded add-ons
