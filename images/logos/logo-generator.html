How to Use This Logo

  1. Click the "Download Logo as PNG" button above to save the logo
  2. Or, take a screenshot:
    • Mac: Press Cmd + Shift + 4, then drag to select the circular logo
    • Windows: Press Win + Shift + S, then select the area
  3. Go back to your Facebook Page
  4. Click the camera icon on your profile picture
  5. Upload the logo image

Note: Facebook will automatically crop this to a circle for your profile picture!
